Who I am

I am docent/universitetslektor (associate professor) at Matematiska Institutionen (Mathematics Department), Uppsala Universitet (Uppsala University).

My research

My research revolves around Dynamical Systems, Hamiltonian systems, Celestial Mechanics, PDEs and Computer-Assisted Proofs. You can find a list my publications here.
